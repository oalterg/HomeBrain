Danger Zone — Nuclear Reset

This performs a complete factory reset. All your data (Nextcloud files, Home Assistant configuration, Vaultwarden vault, OpenClaw memory, AI models, etc.) will be permanently destroyed.

Your external backup drive at /mnt/backup will not be touched. The device will reboot when finished.

This action is irreversible.

This action cannot be undone.

The following will be permanently destroyed:

  • All Nextcloud files and database
  • Home Assistant configuration and history
  • Vaultwarden vault (all saved passwords)
  • OpenClaw memory, chats, and workspace
  • All integration tokens and secrets
  • AI models (unless unchecked below)

Your external backup drive at /mnt/backup will not be touched.

After reset the device will reboot. You will need the factory password on the device label to log in again.
