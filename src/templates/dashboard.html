Recovery Phrase

A short sequence of words that resets your master password if you forget it — your only way back in without wiping the device. It is stored only as a one-way hash, so it can be shown once when generated and never again. Recovery restores admin access to the Dashboard, Nextcloud and Home Assistant; it does not unlock individual Vault items.

Danger zone

Permanently erase all data — Nextcloud files, Home Assistant config, Vault, OpenClaw memory, and AI models. Your backup drive (/mnt/backup) is not affected. The device reboots into a fresh setup.
